Weekend Routine

Time Saturday Time
Morning I eat breakfast and prepare for our rehearsal. - 7:00 AM - 8:00 AM
Afternoon I eat lunch and after that we continue our rehearsal and teach our workshopers. - 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM
Evening After our rehearsal we eat our dinner together and then we get back to our homes. - 7:00 PM - 10:00 PM
Time Sunday Time
Morning I eat breakfast and I prepare for our general rehearsal. - 7:00 AM - 8:00 AM
Afternoon I eat my lunch and we do some exercise or preps for our heavy rehearsal. - 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM
Evening After our general rehearsal we go usually to the coffee shop to unwind and relax from heavy practice, and we go home after that. - 7:00 PM - 10:00 PM
